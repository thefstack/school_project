Welcome

Mission

We are committed to fostering the holistic development of each child, recognizing their unique strengths, interests, and needs

We aim to cultivate a sense of wonder and a lifelong passion for exploration and discovery.

We foster a sense of belonging, respect, and kindness within our school community.

We strive to maintain open lines of communication and create opportunities for parent involvement and engagement in their child's learning journey.

Our school is committed to creating a solid educational foundation that prepares children for a successful transition to Secondary school while nurturing their natural curiosity, love for learning, and joy in the process.

Values

Joy of learning

Cultivating a love for learning by making education enjoyable, engaging, and meaningful for young children.

Creativity

Encouraging children to express themselves, think creatively, and explore their imaginations through various art forms and activities.

Growth Mindset

Instilling a belief in the power of effort, perseverance, and resilience, and encouraging children to embrace challenges and learn from mistakes.

Health and Wellness

Promoting physical health, well-being, and a balanced lifestyle through age-appropriate activities, healthy habits, and nutrition education.

Respect

Promoting respect for oneself, others, and the environment, and fostering an inclusive and diverse community.

Environmental Consciousness

Instilling a sense of environmental responsibility and sustainability, and teaching children to appreciate and care for the natural world.
